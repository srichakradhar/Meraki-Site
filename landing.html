Meraki

/Noun/ Doing something with soul, creativity, or love — when you put “something of yourself” into what you’re doing, whatever it may be.

What do we do?

Meraki is a team of volunteers from the project, who work for employee engagement (or lightening the mood of the project) by regularly conducting a set of activities, like birthday celebrations, bay games, traditional events, etc., which helps the employees to have better interaction, communication and connection with the fellow employees.
